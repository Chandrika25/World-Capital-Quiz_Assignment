World Capitals

Here are some concise notions about each country's capital.

world capitals quiz information:

1.India:

New Delhi, India's capital, is famed for its rich history, diversified culture, and iconic buildings such as the Red Fort and India Gate.

2.United States:

Washington, D.C., the capital of the United States, is known for its historic monuments, federal buildings, and cultural institutions such as the Smithsonian museums.

3.Canada:

Ottawa, Canada's capital, is known for its gorgeous position along the Ottawa River, Parliament Hill, and thriving cultural scene.

4.Australia:

Canberra, the country's capital, is noted for its planned layout, modern architecture, and attractions such as the Australian War Memorial and National Gallery of Australia.

5.Brazil:

Brasília, Brazil's capital, is known for its unique urban design by architect Oscar Niemeyer. Notable modernist buildings include the National Congress and Cathedral of Brasília.

6.South Africa:

Pretoria, one of South Africa's capitals, is known for its jacaranda-lined streets, historical buildings such as the Union Buildings, and diverse cultural heritage.

7.Russia:

Russia's capital, Moscow, is well-known for the Red Square, the Kremlin complex, famous sites including St. Basil's Cathedral, and a thriving nightlife and cultural scene.

8.Argentina:

Argentina's city, Buenos Aires, is renowned for its European-style buildings, fervent tango traditions, and varied food scene.

9.Turkey:

Ankara, Turkey's capital, is known for its modernity, historic sites like the Atatürk Mausoleum, and role as a political and cultural hub in the heart of Anatolia.

10.Egypt:

Cairo, the capital of Egypt, is a city rich in history, home to famous historical landmarks like the Sphinx and Giza Pyramids, busy marketplaces, and a lively street culture along the Nile River.
